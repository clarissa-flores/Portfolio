About Me

Hello! My name is Clarissa, and I am passionate about the intersection of fashion and technology. As a former fashion stylist turned full-stack developer, I bring a unique blend of creative thinking and technical expertise to every project.

My journey into web development began in 2007 when I spent my summer break teaching myself HTML to build a personal homepage, and my love for coding has only grown since then. Through building numerous personal websites and blogs, I've developed a deep understanding of web development and how it's evolved over the years.

With over a decade of experience in retail merchandising, I've honed my skills in optimization and achieved success in fast-paced, collaborative environments. I approach every project with a creative, solution-oriented mindset, and I strive to deliver exceptional results that exceed expectations.
